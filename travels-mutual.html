Roadline Travels Mutual

Effective Date: June 2025

What is Roadline Travels Mutual?

Roadline Travels Mutual is a shared agreement between Roadline, hosts, and guests to promote fair use, shared responsibility, and collaboration in resolving travel-related issues. This framework supports transparency, mutual respect, and cooperation when unexpected events impact a trip.

Key Principles

  • Empathy: Understanding that unforeseen situations (weather delays, illness, family emergencies) may affect plans.
  • Communication: Encouraging proactive contact between host and guest to resolve concerns directly.
  • Good Faith: Assuming positive intentions and engaging constructively when problems arise.
  • Fairness: Applying reasonable solutions when determining compensation, rescheduling, or refunds.

When Does It Apply?

This mutual framework applies in circumstances such as:

  • Flight or train delays or cancellations
  • Sudden medical issues or emergencies
  • Weather-related travel disruptions
  • Unexpected mechanical problems with the vehicle

How It Works

When an issue arises, both parties should:

  • Communicate promptly using in-app messaging or the support line
  • Document the situation (photos, tickets, messages)
  • Attempt to reach a mutual resolution before escalating to Roadline support

Support and Mediation

If a mutual agreement is not reached, Roadline’s support team will mediate and evaluate the situation based on available evidence and mutual terms. Compensation or adjustments may be made according to fairness, not strict liability.

Examples of Resolution

  • A guest provides evidence of a canceled flight and requests to delay pickup; the host agrees to modify the trip without penalty.
  • A host’s vehicle breaks down before the trip, and the guest is offered a full refund or a replacement option.
  • Both guest and host agree to cancel a trip due to weather hazards, and the cancellation is processed without fees.

Limitations

This mutual agreement does not override our Terms of Service or protection policies but adds an extra layer of flexibility and human understanding in applying them.

Our Goal

By participating in Turo Travels Mutual, you help build a community that values fairness, flexibility, and trust — ensuring better outcomes for all.
