Additional Policies

Effective Date: June 2025

Overview

In addition to our core policies, Roadline enforces the following guidelines to ensure clarity, safety, and consistency across the platform.

Late Return Policy

  • Guests must return vehicles by the scheduled time. Delays over 30 minutes may incur additional fees.
  • Hosts may report late returns to receive compensation for extended usage.

Cleaning Policy

  • Guests are expected to return vehicles in reasonably clean condition.
  • Excessive dirt, stains, or odors may lead to cleaning fees charged to the guest.
  • Hosts must provide photographic evidence when requesting cleaning reimbursements.

Fuel Policy

  • Vehicles must be returned with the same level of fuel as when picked up.
  • Refueling fees may apply if the guest returns the vehicle with less fuel than agreed.

Pet Policy

  • Pets are not permitted in vehicles unless explicitly allowed by the host.
  • Guests are responsible for any pet-related damage or cleaning costs.

Smoking Policy

  • Smoking is prohibited in all vehicles unless the host specifies otherwise.
  • Violations may result in fines and cleaning charges.

Lost and Found

Items left in vehicles are the responsibility of the guest. Roadline encourages hosts to report and, if possible, return lost items, but is not liable for personal belongings.

Trip Photos

  • Guests and hosts are encouraged to take pre-trip and post-trip photos to document vehicle condition.
  • Photos serve as protection in case of damage disputes or claims.

Policy Enforcement

Violation of additional policies may result in charges, penalties, or account suspension. Repeat offenses are subject to stricter disciplinary action.
