Community Guidelines

Effective Date: June 2025

Introduction

Roadline’s Community Guidelines are designed to foster a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all users. By participating in our platform, you agree to uphold these standards and contribute positively to the community.

Respect and Safety

  • Treat all users with kindness, respect, and dignity.
  • Avoid discriminatory language or behavior based on race, religion, gender, orientation, nationality, or disability.
  • Do not engage in harassment, threats, or verbal abuse.
  • Respect personal boundaries and privacy.

Integrity and Trust

  • Be honest in your profile information, trip details, and reviews.
  • Only use vehicles with proper authorization and legal documentation.
  • Return vehicles on time and in the agreed condition.
  • Report any incidents or damages immediately and truthfully.

Responsible Usage

  • Operate vehicles safely and in accordance with traffic laws.
  • Do not drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  • Do not use vehicles for prohibited purposes such as racing, off-road driving, or illegal activities.
  • Do not tamper with or modify vehicles without the host’s consent.

Communication

  • Maintain professional and courteous communication at all times.
  • Use in-platform messaging for transparency and support.
  • Do not solicit, spam, or advertise unrelated services to other users.

Platform Conduct

  • Do not manipulate pricing or availability to deceive others.
  • Do not create multiple accounts to bypass rules or restrictions.
  • Do not attempt to conduct transactions outside the platform.

Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience a violation of these guidelines, please report it to our support team immediately. We will investigate all claims and take appropriate action, which may include warnings, suspension, or permanent removal from the platform.

Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines can result in penalties including canceled bookings, withheld earnings, account suspension, or termination. Repeated or severe violations will not be tolerated.

Commitment to Improvement

We are committed to continuously improving our platform. These guidelines may evolve, and we encourage users to check back regularly for updates. Together, we can build a community built on respect, trust, and shared success.
