Redemption

You must have a Roadline account to redeem a Gift Card. Upon redemption, the entire value is added to your account as travel credit, which can be used toward future bookings. If your total exceeds the balance, you’ll need a payment method on file to complete the difference.

No fees apply, and Gift Cards do not expire. However, balances may be reported to governing bodies based on local unclaimed property laws.

Limitations

  • Gift Cards can only be used and gifted within the U.S. (excluding territories).
  • They cannot be resold, reloaded, transferred for value, or redeemed for cash.
