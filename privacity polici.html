Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2025

Introduction

At Roadline, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and the rights you have regarding your data. Our goal is to be transparent and give you control over your personal information when using our platform.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, phone number, government-issued identification (such as driver’s license or passport), and any photos you upload for verification purposes.
  • Usage Data: Information about your interactions with our services, such as login times, page views, device type, browser version, language settings, and IP address.
  • Location Data: GPS and location data if you enable location services or use a GPS-enabled device during a trip.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details, billing address, and payment transaction history. This data is processed securely by our third-party payment processors.
  • Communication Data: Records of messages exchanged through the Roadline platform, emails, and support interactions to ensure safety and resolve issues.
  • Vehicle Data: For hosts, we collect information about your vehicles including make, model, registration, insurance, and service history.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide, personalize, and improve our services.
  • Verify your identity and eligibility to use the platform.
  • Communicate with you about your account, transactions, and important updates.
  • Prevent fraud, ensure safety, and comply with legal obligations.
  • Facilitate insurance and claims processes.
  • Conduct research and analytics to enhance user experience.
  • Send promotional messages and marketing materials (only with your consent).

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data under the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you provide clear consent for us to process your information.
  • Contract: When data processing is necessary to fulfill a contract with you (e.g., booking a trip).
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with laws and regulations.
  • Legitimate Interest: For business operations, fraud prevention, or service improvements that do not override your rights.

Information Sharing

We may share your personal data with:

  • Other users (hosts or guests) to complete trip transactions.
  • Service providers that perform services on our behalf (e.g., payment processors, cloud hosting).
  • Insurance partners for claims and coverage verification.
  • Law enforcement or regulatory bodies when required by law.
  • In case of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

International Transfers

We may transfer your personal data to countries outside your home country. When we do, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, such as Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission or compliance with U.S. privacy frameworks.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal and regulatory obligations, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, and enforcement of our agreements.

Security

We implement advanced security measures such as data encryption, firewalls, access controls, and regular audits to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and obtain a copy of your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of inaccurate or outdated data.
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time without affecting prior processing.
  • Request data portability to another service provider.
  • File a complaint with a data protection authority.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to:

  • Recognize repeat users and personalize content.
  • Monitor site performance and troubleshoot errors.
  • Enable secure login and fraud detection.
  • Deliver targeted ads based on your interests (with your consent).

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, although some features may not function properly without them.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will notify you through the platform or by email. Your continued use of our services after changes have been posted indicates your acceptance of the revised policy.
