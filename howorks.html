Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We’ve got answers.

How do I rent a car with RoadLine?

You just need to register, search for available cars in your city, and book with a few simple steps.

Is it safe to rent from someone else?

Yes! Every driver and car is verified, and we offer full support and insurance on every trip.

Can I cancel my booking?

Absolutely. You can cancel up to 24 hours before the trip starts and get a full refund.

Are there any hidden fees?

Nope. What you see is what you pay. We are 100% transparent.
